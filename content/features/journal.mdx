# Journal & Writing

The journal is the heart of Reflecto. It combines a powerful calendar system, streak tracking, and categorized entries to build your daily reflection habit.

## The Write Page

When you click **Write** in the sidebar, you land on a full-screen, distraction-free writing environment.

### Header Elements

- **Date Display** — Current date in a small mono-spaced label, with the day of the week rendered in large serif italic typography
- **Special Date Badges** — On holidays or observances, a pulsing sparkle badge appears (configurable in Settings)
- **Bullet Count** — When bulleted mode is enabled, shows the number of bullet points in your entry as `#42`
- **Cloud Sync Indicator** — Real-time sync status with a green checkmark when saved
- **Star Toggle** — Click the star icon to mark any entry as important

### Entry Types

The Write page supports all six entry types. Pass `?type=journal` (or `dream`, `highlight`, `idea`, `wisdom`, `note`) in the URL to switch, or create entries from their respective workspace pages.

## Journal Calendar

The **Journal** page displays your entries organized by date with two navigation modes:

### Calendar View
A full monthly calendar showing:
- **Entry indicators** — Dots on dates that have entries
- **Date selection** — Click any date to view entries from that day
- **Month navigation** — Arrow buttons to move between months

### Calendar Strip
A compact horizontal date strip for quick week-at-a-glance navigation.

## Entry Categories

Journal entries can be categorized to organize your reflections:

| Category | Use For |
|----------|---------|
| **Thoughts** | General musings and ideas |
| **Involved Me** | Events you were part of |
| **Around Me** | Things happening in your surroundings |
| **Memories** | Past experiences worth recording |
| **Gratitude** | Things you're thankful for |
| **Reflections** | Deep introspection |
| **Tiny Wins** | Small victories worth celebrating |
| **Daily Log** | Factual account of your day |
| **Venting** | Getting things off your chest |

<Callout type="info">
Categories are optional. You can also create custom categories — the field accepts free-form text.
</Callout>

## Mood Tracking

Each journal entry supports a **mood score** from 1 to 5, set through the metadata editor. This data flows into your Insights dashboard to show mood trends over time.

## Streak Tracking

Reflecto tracks your **writing streak** — the number of consecutive days you've created at least one entry.

- **Streak Bar** — Displayed on the journal page showing your current streak length
- **Active Streak** — Maintained in the database with start date, end date, and length
- **Activity Logs** — Daily entry counts are tracked per entry type for the heatmap and charts

## Starring Entries

Click the star icon on any entry to mark it as important. Starred entries can be filtered and appear with a gold highlight across list views.

## Linked Context

When editing an existing entry, the **Linked Context** section appears below the editor, showing related entries from the same date. This helps you see the full picture of that day's reflections.
