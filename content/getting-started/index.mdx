## Quick Start Checklist

Follow these steps to get up and running:

- [ ] **Create your account** — Sign up with email or OAuth
- [ ] **Verify your email** — Check your inbox and activate your account
- [ ] **Write your first entry** — Capture your thoughts on the Write page
- [ ] **Explore entry types** — Try Dreams, Highlights, Ideas, Wisdom, or Notes
- [ ] **Set up tags & people** — Use @mentions and #tags to connect your thoughts
- [ ] **Customize your experience** — Adjust settings for appearance and preferences
- [ ] **Check Insights** — View your writing patterns and streaks

## What Makes Reflecto Special?

Reflecto is designed to be more than just a digital notebook. Here's what sets it apart:

### 🔒 Privacy First
Your reflections are personal. All data is stored securely with authentication and encryption built in from day one.

### ✨ Beautiful Writing Experience
A dark-first, premium aesthetic with serif typography, smooth animations, and intentional whitespace makes writing feel special.

### 🔗 Connected Thinking
Tags, people mentions, and insights link your entries together, revealing patterns you'd never notice in a traditional journal.

### 📊 Insightful Analytics
Activity heatmaps, writing streaks, mood trends, and entry statistics help you understand your reflection habits over time.

### 🎨 Rich Editor
TipTap-powered editor with markdown support, formatting options, image uploads, @mentions, and #tags.

### 🌙 Six Entry Types
Tailor your reflections with specialized workspaces: Journal, Dreams, Highlights, Ideas, Wisdom, and Notes.
