# Installation & Setup

Get your Reflecto account set up and ready to use. This guide covers account creation, email verification, and initial configuration.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A valid email address for account creation
- Optionally: A Google or GitHub account for OAuth sign-in

<Callout type="info">
Reflecto is a web application — no installation required! Simply access it through your browser.
</Callout>

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
### Navigate to Reflecto

Open your browser and go to the Reflecto homepage. You'll see the landing page with a **Sign Up** button in the header.

### Choose Your Sign-Up Method

Reflecto offers multiple ways to create an account:

<Tabs items={['Email & Password', 'OAuth (Google/GitHub)']}>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    **Email & Password Sign-Up**
    
    1. Click **Sign Up** in the header
    2. Fill in the registration form:
       - **Email address** — Your primary email
       - **Password** — Choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
       - **Confirm password** — Re-enter your password
    3. Click **Create Account**
    4. Check your email for a verification link
    
    <Callout type="warning">
    Passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt. Make sure to use a unique, strong password.
    </Callout>
  </Tabs.Tab>
  <Tabs.Tab>
    **OAuth Sign-Up**
    
    1. Click **Sign Up** in the header
    2. Choose your preferred provider:
       - **Continue with Google** — Use your Google account
       - **Continue with GitHub** — Use your GitHub account
    3. Authorize Reflecto to access your basic profile information
    4. You'll be automatically signed in — no email verification needed
    
    <Callout type="info">
    OAuth is powered by NextAuth and only accesses your email and basic profile. No password is stored.
    </Callout>
  </Tabs.Tab>
</Tabs>

### Verify Your Email (Email Sign-Up Only)

If you signed up with email and password:

1. Check your inbox for an email from Reflecto
2. Open the email and click the **Verify Email** button
3. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page
4. Enter your credentials and sign in

<Callout type="warning">
Can't find the verification email? Check your spam folder. If it's still missing, request a new verification link from the sign-in page.
</Callout>

### Complete Your Profile

After signing in for the first time, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:

1. **Display Name** (optional) — How you'd like to be addressed in the app
2. **Profile Image** (optional) — Upload an avatar or use your OAuth provider's image

These can be updated later in Settings.

### You're In!

🎉 Your account is now active! You'll be redirected to the **Write** page, ready to create your first entry.
</Steps>

## Initial Configuration

After signing in, take a moment to configure your preferences:

### Access Settings

Click your profile avatar in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then select **Settings**.

### Recommended Settings to Review

| Setting | Location | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Appearance** | Settings → Appearance | Adjust theme, font size, and editor mode preference |
| **Special Dates** | Settings → Preferences | Toggle holiday badges and sparkle indicators |
| **Auto-Hide Navigation** | Settings → Preferences | Set sidebar behavior for distraction-free writing |
| **Default Entry Type** | Settings → Preferences | Choose what type loads on the Write page |
| **Bulleted Mode** | Settings → Preferences | Enable bullet-point style entries by default |

<Callout type="info">
All settings are stored per-user and sync across devices if you sign in from multiple locations.
</Callout>

## Security & Privacy

Reflecto takes your privacy seriously:

- ✅ **Secure Authentication** — Email/password with bcrypt hashing, or OAuth via NextAuth
- ✅ **Session Management** — Secure session tokens with automatic expiration
- ✅ **Data Encryption** — All data transmitted over HTTPS
- ✅ **Private by Default** — Only you can see your entries
- ✅ **No Third-Party Tracking** — Your data stays with Reflecto

### Managing Your Account

- **Change Password** — Settings → Security (email accounts only)
- **Sign Out** — Click your profile avatar → Sign Out
- **Delete Account** — Contact support (feature coming soon)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't sign in?

- **Check your credentials** — Ensure email and password are correct
- **Verify your email** — Email accounts must be verified before first sign-in
- **Clear browser cache** — Try signing in from an incognito/private window
- **Reset password** — Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page

### OAuth Issues

- **Authorization failed** — Check that you granted Reflecto access to your email
- **Account already exists** — If you previously signed up with email, use that method instead
- **Redirect error** — Ensure JavaScript and cookies are enabled in your browser

### Still Having Issues?

Reflecto is in alpha and we're actively fixing bugs. If you encounter persistent issues:

- Check the browser console for error messages
- Try a different browser
- Clear your browser's cache and cookies
