# Getting Started

Get up and running with Reflecto in minutes. This guide walks you through account creation, your first entry, and navigating the app.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
### Sign Up

Navigate to the Reflecto homepage and click **Sign Up**. You can create an account using:
- **Email & Password** — Standard credentials with secure bcrypt hashing
- **OAuth** — Sign in with your existing Google or GitHub account via NextAuth

### Verify Your Email

If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a verification link. Click it to activate your account.

### You're In!

After signing in, you'll land on the **Write** page — ready to start capturing your thoughts.
</Steps>

## Your First Entry

The **Write** page is your primary workspace. Here's what you'll see:

- **Date Header** — Today's date displayed in an elegant serif font
- **Entry Type** — Defaults to "Journal". You can switch between Journal, Dream, Highlight, Idea, Wisdom, or Note
- **Rich Editor** — Start typing. The editor supports markdown, formatting, and more
- **Star Button** — Mark important entries with a star for quick access later
- **Cloud Sync** — Your entry auto-saves. Look for the green checkmark to confirm

<Callout type="info">
On special dates (holidays, observances), you'll see a sparkle badge next to the date. This can be toggled in Settings.
</Callout>

## Navigating the App

The sidebar on the left is your navigation hub, split into two sections:

### Primary Navigation
| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Write** | Create or edit entries |
| **Journal** | Browse entries by calendar |
| **Reflect** | Timeline view of recent activity |
| **Insights** | Analytics and charts |

### Categories
| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Dreams** | Future aspirations |
| **Highlights** | Meaningful moments |
| **Tags** | Manage your tags |
| **People** | Manage mentioned people |
| **Notes** | Quick notes |
| **Wisdom** | Collected wisdom |
| **Ideas** | Ideas and brainstorms |

<Callout type="info">
The sidebar can be collapsed on desktop and auto-hides on mobile. You can also enable **Auto-Hide Navigation** in Settings for a cleaner look.
</Callout>
