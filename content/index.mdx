# Welcome to Reflecto

**Reflecto** is a private, AI-powered journaling app designed for mindful daily reflection. Built for people who want a beautiful, distraction-free space to capture thoughts, track patterns, and grow through writing.

## What is Reflecto?

Reflecto isn't just a note-taking app — it's a **reflection ecosystem**. Every entry you write becomes part of a connected web of thoughts, people, tags, and insights that help you understand yourself better over time.

> **Reflecto is currently in Alpha.** We're actively building and refining the experience. Expect frequent updates and new features.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Rich Editor** | A TipTap-powered editor with markdown, formatting, @mentions, #tags, and image uploads |
| **Journal** | Daily journal entries with categories, mood tracking, and calendar navigation |
| **Future Dreams** | Capture your aspirations and long-term visions |
| **Highlights** | Record meaningful moments with importance levels |
| **Ideas** | Track ideas from seed to draft with status progression |
| **Wisdom** | Collect quotes, thoughts, facts, excerpts, and life lessons |
| **Notes** | Quick notes with pinning and color coding |
| **Insights** | Activity heatmaps, streaks, stats, and charts across all entry types |
| **Tags & People** | Auto-extracted from entries, with grouping and management |

## Design Philosophy

Reflecto is built on three principles:

1. **Privacy First** — Your reflections are personal. Authentication and secure data handling are built in from day one.
2. **Beautiful Minimalism** — A dark-first, premium aesthetic that makes writing feel special. Serif headings, smooth animations, and intentional whitespace.
3. **Connected Reflection** — Tags, people, and insights link your entries together, revealing patterns you'd never notice in a traditional journal.
